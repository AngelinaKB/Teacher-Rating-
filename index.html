FAQ

+

What is the purpose of this site?

Rate My Teacher is about helping students answer a single question — what do I need to know to maximize my chance of success in a given class? Rate My Teacher lets users share experiences about how teachers and professors teach their classes. Students are not alike, and what may prove challenging for one student may be facile for another. .

+

How do I leave my review?

Students can select any semester of their choice from the semesters given and then select the particular teacher they with to leave a review for.

+

What do teachers and professors think of this site?

Well, that depends on the teacher! Before relaunching we received a lot of emails from teachers telling us they like the site, and we've even had some telling us how the site has helped them. We also received some pretty negative emails opposing the idea.

+

Why was my rating removed?

Ratings are sometimes removed due to improper comments, but more commonly they are removed after having been identified as originating from the same source (spamming of ratings, also called “astroturfing” ). Remember, rate each teacher or professor only once!

+

Will my teacher know whether I left a review for them?

No, your teacher will not be notifed who left a review for them. Only the administrator will be aware of who posted the reviews.
